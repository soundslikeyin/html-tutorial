My favourite places in Okinawa

  • Zamami
  • Tokashiki
  • Odo Beach

Zamami

Known for the beautiful lookouts, Zamami offers more than just a beautiful beach to relax on.
For those keen to explore, there are little islands surrounding Zamami that you can visit where lots of sealife can be found underwater

Tokashiki

Less popular amongst tourists, which means more peace and quiet, is the island Tokashiki.

Odo Beach

A favourite of locals in Itoman, is the very unassuming Odo Beach.
You may encounter swarms of fishes if you dip inside in the morning
